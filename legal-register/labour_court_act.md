# Legal Register: Labour Court Act

## Purpose
ใช้กำหนดว่าประเด็นใดอยู่ในขอบเขตคดีแรงงานและควรค้นแนวคำพิพากษาศาลฎีกาด้านแรงงาน

## Typical labour court issues
- Rights and duties under employment contract
- Rights and duties under working condition agreement
- Labour protection disputes
- Labour relations disputes
- Social security disputes
- Workmen compensation disputes

## Agent note
Before searching, classify whether the issue is a labour case, civil employment dispute, administrative issue, or criminal issue.
